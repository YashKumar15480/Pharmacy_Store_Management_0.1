1. Introduction
1.1 Project Description
The PSM has been developed to override the problems prevailing in the practicing manual system. This
software is supported to eliminate and in some cases reduce the hardships by the manual records
maintaining of inventory using papers. Moreover this is designed for the particular need of the store to
carry out operations in a smooth and effective manner.
The application is reduced as much as possible to avoid errors while entering the data. It also provide error
messages while entering invalid data. No formal knowledge is needed for the user to use this system.
Thus by this all it proves, it is a user-friendly Inventory Management software. It assist the user to
concentrate on their other activities rather to concentrate on the record keeping. Thus it will help
organization in better utilization of resources.
Following are the functionalities of our project:
• Manage the information of inventory
• Manage the information of cost
• Editing and Updating of records is improved which results in proper resource management
• Provides the searching facility based on inventory name, dealer name etc
• Shows the complete information of inventory
1.2 Project Scope
The main purpose of the project on Inventory Management was to reduce paper work and support
digitalization. It may help collecting perfect management in details. In a very short time, collection will be
obvious, simple and sensible. It will help a person to know the management of passed records perfectly
and vividly.
Our project aims at Business process automation, i.e., we have tried to computerised varioud processes of
Inventory Management.
• In computer system, it is not necessary to create the manifest but we can directly print it,
which saves our time.
• To assist the staff in capturing the effort spent on their respective working areas.
• To utilize resources in an efficient manner by increasing their productivity through
automation
• Be easy to operate
• It satisfy the user requirement
• Have a good user interface
• Be expandable
• Manage the information of cost
• Editing and Updating of records is improved which results in proper resource management

2. Feasibility Study
A feasibility analysis is used to determine the viability of an idea, such as ensuring a project is
legally and technically feasible as well as economically justifiable
This section is the evaluation of a proposed project to determine if its technically feasible,
feasible within estimated cost, and will be profitable. We, the team members have created the
following feasibility analysis for the Pharmacy Store Management.
2.1 Technical Feasibility
Technical feasibility is the process of figuring out how we're going to produce our
product or service to determine whether it's possible for our company.
Our project, the Pharmacy Store Management is feasible technically because of the
following reasons:
• The technologies we are using are updated versions.
• Technologies are supporting modern OS and commonly used ones.
• There is no need to install any supporting technology
2.2 Economical Feasibility
Technical feasibility is the process of figuring out whether our project is economically
feasible or not. It is a kind of cost-benefit analysis of the examined project, which assesses
whether it is possible to implement it. In the following ways we can check economical feasibility
of our project:
• Whether the technologies we are using are freely available or not?
• Depending on the number of members and time duration for making this project,
defines project cost as well
2.3 Operational Feasibility
Operational feasibility is the measure of how well a proposed system solves the
problems, and takes advantage of the opportunities identified during scope definition and how it
satisfies the requirements identified in the requirements analysis phase of system development.
• Our Project is used for maintaining inventory records in digital form at any
pharmacy store.
• This projects supports digitalization
2.4 Behavioral Feasibility
Behavioral Feasibility is the measure of how the society is looking towards our project,
what is the reaction of people who are going to use this in upcoming future.
It includes how strong the reaction of user will be towards the development of new
system that involves computer’s use in their daily life for maintaining digital records of
inventory at pharmacy store.
